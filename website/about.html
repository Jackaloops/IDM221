About Us

Full Moon Studios is a Philadelphia-based independent game studio founded in 2015 by three awkward Drexel undergrads who simply really, really enjoyed gaming. In time that enthusiasm grew into a decision to strike new ground and create their own mark on their beloved hobby, and Full Moon Studios was born.

FMS is currently developing its World War Wolves title for PC and Android. For more information feel free to email us at contact@fullmoongamestudios.com.
