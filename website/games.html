World War Wolves

World War Wolves is a fantasy war simulator based around the idea of canine husbandry. Inspired by titles such as the Fire Emblem series, Spore, and Battle Cats, WWW seeks a lighthearted but complex approach to the traditional war game genre.

Taking place in a future where wolves have become America's dominant species, the player is put in the role of general commander and tasked to dominate the world.
