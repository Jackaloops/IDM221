World War Wolves

World War Wolves is a fantasy war simulator based around the idea of canine husbandry. Inspired by titles such as the Fire Emblem series, Spore, and No Man's Sky, WWW seeks a lighthearted but complex approach to the traditional war game genre.

Taking place in a future where wolves and canines gained intelligence and took over the world, the player is put in the role of general commander and tasked to dominate the galaxy.
